<h3><strong>MOE’s Parents Gateway Mobile App</strong></h3>
<p></p>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 25%;" height="auto" width="100%" alt="" src="/images/For Parents/PG_Icon.jpg">
</div>
<p>Our school will communicate with parents through MOE’s Parents Gateway
(PG) Mobile App, a digital platform developed by Ministry of Education
and GovTech. With the free app, parents will be able to receive timely
information about school activities/events (e.g. enrichment, CCA, etc)
via push notification. In addition, you can also give consent for your
child’s activities via the app.</p>
<p>If you have children in different schools, you will be able to receive
notifications on the same PG App. Parents can download the PG App individually
to receive notices.</p>
<p>You will need to download the PG app onto your mobile phone (available
on iOS and Andriod platforms) and do a one-time registration (refer to
Instructional Guide for the step-by-step guide). Please note that the login
is via SingPass. If you are unable to access the PG App, please inform
your child’s Form Teacher and he/she will assist you.</p>

</p>
