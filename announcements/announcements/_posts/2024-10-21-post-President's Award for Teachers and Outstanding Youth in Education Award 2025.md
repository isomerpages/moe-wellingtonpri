<p>Dear Parents / Guardians,</p>
<p>The nominations for the President’s Award for Teachers (PAT) and the Outstanding
Youth in Education Award (OYEA) 2025 are open. These awards are given out
to teachers to recognise their professional excellence.</p>
<p>The President’s Award for Teachers (PAT) recognises experienced educators
who demonstrate a deep passion for teaching and inspire both their students
and peers through their words and deeds. Please submit your nominations
for this award at <a href="https://go.gov.sg/pat2025" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">https://go.gov.sg/pat2025</a>
</p><a class="isomer-image-wrapper" href="https://go.gov.sg/pat2025"><img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="" src="/images/PAT_2025.jpg"></a>
<p></p>
<p>The Outstanding Youth in Education Award (OYEA) on the other hand honours
the achievements of young educators who have shown admirable passion for
teaching and commitment to inspiring and nurturing their students. Please
take note that the OYEA is open to educators who are 35 years old and below
in 2025<strong>. </strong>You may submit your nominations for this award
at <a href="https://go.gov.sg/oyea2025" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">https://go.gov.sg/oyea2025</a>
</p>
<p></p><a class="isomer-image-wrapper" href="https://go.gov.sg/oyea2025"><img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="" src="/images/2025_OYEA.jpg"></a>
<p>Nominations for both awards will close on 10 January 2025, Friday.</p>
<p>Thank you.</p>
