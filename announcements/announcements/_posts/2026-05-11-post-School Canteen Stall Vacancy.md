<p></p>
<ol start="5" data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Interested applicants must meet the following criteria:</p>
</li>
</ol>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Be a Singapore Citizen or Singapore Permanent Resident for stallholder</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Able to provide good service and quality food with prices determined in
consultation with the school</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Able to provide cooked food that meets the health / nutritional standards
set by the Health Promotion Board (HPB) under the "Healthy Meals in Schools
Programme (HMSP)" guidelines (see link – link to <a href="https://www.hpb.gov.sg/schools/school-programmes/healthy-meals-in-schools-programme" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">Healthy Meals in Schools Programme</a>).</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Able to maintain a high standard of food safety and personal hygiene</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Be healthy and fit to operate the stall</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Must be in personal attendance at the stall</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<ol start="6" data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Shortlisted applicants will be notified for an interview and will be required
to provide food sampling for the School Canteen Committee at the applicant's
own expense.</p>
</li>
</ol>
